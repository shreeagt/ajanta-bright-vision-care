Our Doctors

Welcome to Bright Vision Care, where a team of proficient and empathetic doctors, led by Dr. Rakheee Khatiwala, come together to provide exceptional eye care services. Situated in Surat, our hospital is a hub of expertise in ophthalmology. Our doctors, driven by a passion for enhancing vision and promoting eye aesthetics, collaborate seamlessly to deliver individualized and comprehensive care to each patient. At Bright Vision Care, your eye health and visual satisfaction are our top priorities.

Dr. Rakheee Khatiwala

Meet Dr. Rakheee Khatiwala, a distinguished expert in the field of Ophthalmology based in Surat. With a solid background in medicine and an impressive specialization in M.S. Ophthalmology, Dr. Khatiwala has established herself as a dedicated and compassionate eye care professional.

At her esteemed hospital in Surat, Dr. Khatiwala brings a unique blend of expertise and empathy to her patients. Her primary focus lies in enhancing the aesthetic appeal of the eyes while ensuring optimal visual functionality. With a meticulous approach, she strives to help her clients achieve not only improved vision but also a more aesthetically pleasing appearance.

Dr. Khatiwala's commitment to her patients goes beyond clinical expertise. She takes the time to understand each individual's needs and concerns, tailoring her treatments to address both medical and cosmetic aspects of eye care. Through her skilled hands and advanced techniques, she aims to make a positive impact on her patients' lives by providing them with the gift of clear vision and enhanced eye aesthetics.

Common Diseases

Age Related Macular Degeneration

Progressive vision loss in the center of the visual field as you age
Cataracts

Clouding of the eye's natural lens, leading to blurry vision
Diabetic Retinopathy

Damage to the retina due to diabetes, potentially leading to vision loss
Glaucoma

Eye condition characterized by increased intraocular pressure, damaging the optic nerve
Pterygium

Abnormal growth of tissue on the eye's surface, often caused by sun exposure
